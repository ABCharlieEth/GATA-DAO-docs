# What are the benefits of holding GATA NFT?

Holding a GATA NFT gives you the following utility:

**路**        Voting power in GATA DAO

**路**        50% Validator commission distributed to GATA holders

**路**       50% Royalties from secondary market distributed to GATA holders

**路**        50% DAO Treasury ownership in proportion to your NFT ownership

9% Minting revenue from each of future GATA collections to be distributed to all GATA holders.
